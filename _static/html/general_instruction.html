Instruction: Personalized Stock Assessment Stage

It is December 31, 2023. You are an investor exploring individual stocks and have come to consult a financial advisor for guidance. In this experiment, you’ll choose a stock from the available tabs and discuss its suitability with the advisor. If you encounter any unfamiliar financial concepts or need clarification, feel free to ask questions.

Note: The candidate stock is randomly selected and is not a recommendation from the advisor.

To begin, click the Start Conversation button, type your message in the User Input box, and press Send This Message to Advisor. If the advisor’s response is truncated, click Show More of the Advisor’s Answer to continue.

Evaluation Instructions

Welcome to the evaluation process! Please follow the steps below to complete your evaluation:

  1. Evaluation Criteria: Please evaluate the system in the following aspects:
    • Likelihood of Purchase: Rate the likelihood of purchase.
    • Reason: Write down the reason for your choice in the free text box labeled "The reason of your choice".
    • Confidence in Your Decision: Rate the confidence in your decision.
    • Familiarity Level: Rate your familiarity with the stock before the interaction.
  2. After completing all the evaluation criteria, click the "Send: Evaluation" button to submit your responses. Once you submit, you cannot resubmit, so please review your answers carefully before submitting.

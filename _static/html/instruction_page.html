Welcome to the FinPersona Demonstration!

Please read the instructions carefully before beginning.

Step 1: Personality Injection

In this initial step, you will choose the personality style for your financial advisor. Select either the Optimist (Friendly, Daring, Confident) or the Pragmatist (Disciplined, Cautious, Practical) option. This choice will set the tone and approach of your advisor for the entire demonstration.

Step 2: User Preference Elicitation

During this stage, the financial advisors will ask you a series of questions to gather insights into your investment preferences and needs. The information you provide will help guide the discussions and decisions in the subsequent stages. Review the assigned user narrative thoroughly and role-play as the investor described.

Step 3: Personalized Stock Assessment

You will go through four rounds of financial discussions. Please move through the following sequence: 1: Discuss → 2: Discuss → … → 4: Discuss.

Discussion Phase (e.g., 1: Discuss)

You will receive general instructions and information about a candidate stock, which has been randomly selected for this exploration. After reviewing the instructions, engage with the financial advisor to make your decisions. Once you feel that you have gathered enough information, proceed to the evaluation phase. Note that the candidate stock is not a recommendation from the advisor.

Next Round (e.g., 2: Discuss)

After evaluating one stock, move to the next tab and repeat the process until all four stocks have been assessed.

Cautionary Guidelines

  • Do not refresh or reload the page, as this will result in loss of data and require you to restart the demonstration.

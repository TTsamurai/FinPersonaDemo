Welcome to the FinPersona Demonstration!

Please read the instructions carefully before beginning.

Step 1: Personality Injection

Ready to begin? Select your financial advisor’s style: Optimist (Friendly, Daring, Confident) or Pragmatist (Disciplined, Cautious, Practical). Your choice sets the tone for the entire FinPersona demo!

Step 2: User Preference Elicitation

It’s question time! Your financial advisors need a snapshot of your investment needs and preferences. Feel free to use the sample user narrative or just answer as yourself—either way, your input will shape the next steps. Let’s dive in!

Step 3: Personalized Stock Assessment

You’ll engage in financial discussions with an LLM-powered advisor. Pick one stock you’d like to explore—keeping in mind, this choice is not an official recommendation from the advisor.

Cautionary Guidelines

Do not refresh or reload the page, as this will result in loss of data and require you to restart the demonstration.
